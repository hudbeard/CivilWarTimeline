Civil War

Nov 1860

Lincoln Elected

Republican Abraham Lincoln was elected president in the 1860 election, campaining to halt the spread of slavery. He did not wish to abolish it, simply to put it in check.

March 1861

Lincoln's Inauguration

Lincoln gives his Inaugural Address, making his intentions about slavery known to the nation. He also forbid states from leaving the union in an attempt to keep the U.S. united

Jan 1863

Emancipation Proclaimation

Union finaly gets a victory at Antietam, albiet small. President Lincoln issues the Emancipation Proclaimation, which changed the terms of war. The North now fights for union and liberty.

Feb 1861

A Nation Divides

In the span of a few months, seven southern states seceded from the United States with four more to follow. Many in the north felt that the U.S. should allow the states to withdrawal as a form of national clensing.

April 1861

Battle of Fort Sumter

Confederate Forces attack federal troops before they can be reinforced. Because the south started the war, Lincoln was given the moral high ground.
